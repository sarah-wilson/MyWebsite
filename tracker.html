What is this?

The yellow blobs in the above are where I have taken a photo. I was in a boat, don't be alarmed that many of them are in the middle of the sea.

This is a project I started in 2018 and update about once a year. I think I use it remind myself why I am not a software developer, because updating it is always full of dramas. Most recently updated January 2023 to include a trip to Rwanda.

It is better on a computer than a mobile but you can zoom in and move around.
