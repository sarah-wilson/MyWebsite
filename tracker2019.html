Trip Tracker 2019

What is this?

It is a map of places where I've come across something that I've decided was worth capturing. I have always taken a lot of photos and I wanted a project, so this seemed like a good idea.

You can explore the map. There are locations tagged in SE Asia and Europe. It is better on a computer than a mobile.
